Founder's Message

The Edutop School of Excellence, Rahata was established in June 2014 with the blessings of Late Freedom Fighter Karbhari Shashande Patil and the wholehearted support of socially conscious citizens.

What began with only 44 students and 4 teachers has grown into a thriving institution with over 580 learners and modern infrastructure. Each year, the school added new grades, expanded facilities, and strengthened its commitment to quality English-medium education rooted in values.

Guided by the ideals of Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil, we continue to believe that education should be accessible, value-based, and transformative — shaping our students into responsible and compassionate citizens of tomorrow.

Principal's Message

Welcome to Edutop School of Excellence, where we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also character development. Our commitment is to provide a holistic education that prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow while instilling values that last a lifetime.

About Edutop School of Excellence

Established in 1985, Edutop School of Excellence has been a beacon of quality education for over three decades. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can explore their potential and develop into well-rounded individuals.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character in our students, preparing them to be responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that inspires excellence and innovation while preserving our cultural values and traditions.
